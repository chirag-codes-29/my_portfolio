About

Someone who ends up holding a lot together.

I'm Chirag, a first year IT student at Delhi Technological University. I tend to get involved in a lot of different things at once, and somehow keep them all moving.

On campus, I'm the Class Representative for my department, acting as the bridge between 70+ classmates and faculty, and part of DTU's Placement Committee. At MACS, I've worked on event moderation, PR, and sponsorship outreach. I'm also a social volunteer at Rotaract Club of DTU Regency, an outreach associate at STEP DTU, and a fashion model at Glykeria, DTU's fashion society.

I was selected for the Dell Aspire Scholar Program, a merit-based initiative by Dell Technologies. Something I genuinely want to build toward eventually is agriculture — I come from a background deeply connected to farming, and I'd love to bring IT and AI into that space in a way that actually helps people.
